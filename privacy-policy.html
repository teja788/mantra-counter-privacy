Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This app collects no data. Your practice is entirely private.

What We Collect

Nothing. Japa Mala does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal data is collected or processed
  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking of any kind
  • No advertising or ad-related data collection
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data are included
  • No network requests are made — the app works entirely offline
  • No accounts, logins, or registration required

Data Storage

All app data — your mala count, session history, practice streak, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only using standard on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Is not accessible to the developer or anyone else
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

Permissions

The app requests only the VIBRATE permission to provide haptic feedback as you count each bead. This permission does not access any personal data.

Internet Access

This app does not require an internet connection and makes no network calls. It functions fully offline. No data is sent to or received from any server.

Children's Privacy

Since this app collects no data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We comply with COPPA and similar regulations by virtue of collecting nothing.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this same URL with an updated date. Since we collect no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us by opening an issue on the app's GitHub repository.
